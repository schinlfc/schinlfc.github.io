##### Abstract

This study aims to analyze recent trends in the consumption of kratom drinks and assess their potential public health implications. A retrospective observational approach was employed, utilizing content analysis of Reddit posts discussing kratom. Data were gathered from the top 20 kratom-related subreddits using the PRAW package in Python. Posts and comments containing keywords associated with kratom drinks were extracted and aggregated on a monthly basis. To account for the growth in Reddit user base over time, the number of posts was normalized accordingly. The analysis identified a marked increase in interest in kratom drinks beginning in mid-2022, which correlates with their expanded availability in vape shops, bars, convenience stores, and online platforms. These findings suggest a growing concern regarding the public health impact of kratom drink consumption. The study underscores the importance of raising public awareness about the potential risks associated with these products.
